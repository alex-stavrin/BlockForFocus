# BlockForFocus

BlockForFocus is a sleek, modern, and effective Chrome Extension designed to help you reclaim your time and stay locked in on your work. 

Whether you're studying for an exam, writing a paper, or just trying to get through your daily tasks, the internet is full of distractions. BlockForFocus helps you build better habits by physically preventing you from accessing those distractions when you need to be productive.

## 🎯 How It Works

1. **Set Your Timer & Subject:** Open the extension, select a custom subject, and set how long you want to focus (e.g., 25 minutes for a Pomodoro session).
2. **Start Focusing:** Once you click start, BlockForFocus activates. Any tabs you already have open that match your blocklist will be instantly blocked!
3. **Stay Locked In:** Try to open Twitter, Discord, or any site you've added to your blocklist. Instead of doomscrolling, you'll instantly be met with a strict, distraction-free screen reminding you to get back to work.
4. **Track Your Wins:** When the timer finishes, you'll hear a chime and receive a notification celebrating your success! You can view your detailed focus history and completed sessions in the Stats tab.

## ✨ Features

- **Custom Subjects:** Categorize your focus time! Create custom subjects with personalized color badges (e.g., "Mathematics", "Coding", "Reading") to track exactly what you've been working on.
- **Detailed History & Stats:** Keep yourself motivated by reviewing a detailed log of your recent sessions, complete with start/end times, subjects, and durations. Track your daily total focus minutes over the last 30 days.
- **Manual Time Management:** Forgot to start the timer while you were working? No problem. Manually add or remove time to keep your daily stats accurate.
- **Custom Blocklists with Favicons:** Easily add or remove websites from your blocklist directly in the extension. The blocklist automatically fetches and displays the official favicon for each site you add.
- **Instant Tab Blocking:** Built on modern browser architecture (Manifest V3), sites are blocked instantly without any annoying buffering. Furthermore, the moment you start a session, any open tabs matching your blocklist are immediately blocked!
- **Keyboard Friendly:** Power user? You can press `Enter` in almost every input field to quickly add sites, subjects, or start timers.
- **Pause & Resume:** Life happens. If you need to step away for an emergency, you can pause your session and resume it later.

## 🔒 100% Private & Local

Your privacy is paramount. **BlockForFocus operates entirely locally on your machine.** 

- We do not collect your browsing history.
- We do not track what sites you block.
- We do not require an account or login.
- **All your focus statistics, subjects, and settings are saved locally on your own hard drive.**

Your focus journey is yours alone. 

## 🚀 Getting Started

To install the extension locally:
1. Open Google Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Enable **Developer mode** in the top right corner.
3. Click **Load unpacked** in the top left.
4. Select the folder containing this project.
5. Pin the extension to your toolbar, set your timer, and get to work!
