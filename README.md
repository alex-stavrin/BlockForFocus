# BlockForFocus

BlockForFocus is a sleek, simple, and effective Chrome Extension designed to help you reclaim your time and stay locked in on your work. 

Whether you're studying for an exam, writing a paper, or just trying to get through your daily tasks, the internet is full of distractions. BlockForFocus helps you build better habits by physically preventing you from accessing those distractions when you need to be productive.

## 🎯 How It Works

1. **Set Your Timer:** Open the extension and set how long you want to focus (e.g., 25 minutes for a Pomodoro session).
2. **Start Focusing:** Once you click start, BlockForFocus activates. 
3. **Stay Locked In:** Try to open Twitter, Discord, or any site you've added to your blocklist. Instead of doomscrolling, you'll instantly be met with a clean, distraction-free screen reminding you to get back to work.
4. **Track Your Wins:** When the timer finishes, you'll hear a chime and receive a notification celebrating your success! You can view your total focus time and completed sessions in the Stats tab.

## ✨ Features

- **Custom Blocklists:** Easily add or remove websites from your blocklist directly in the extension.
- **Beautiful User Interface:** Enjoy a smooth, modern design with a calming color palette and an intuitive layout.
- **Instant Blocking:** Built on modern browser architecture, sites are blocked instantly without any annoying buffering or loading screens.
- **Focus Statistics:** Keep yourself motivated by tracking how many sessions you've completed and how many total minutes you've spent focusing.
- **Pause & Resume:** Life happens. If you need to step away for an emergency, you can pause your session and resume it later.

## 🔒 100% Private & Local

Your privacy is paramount. **BlockForFocus operates entirely locally on your machine.** 

- We do not collect your browsing history.
- We do not track what sites you block.
- We do not require an account or login.
- **All your focus statistics and settings are saved locally on your own hard drive.**

Your focus journey is yours alone. 

## 🚀 Getting Started

To install the extension locally:
1. Open Google Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Enable **Developer mode** in the top right corner.
3. Click **Load unpacked** in the top left.
4. Select the folder containing this project.
5. Pin the extension to your toolbar, set your timer, and get to work!
